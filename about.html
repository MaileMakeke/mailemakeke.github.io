About us

Welcome to Maile Mākeke! A website for locally sourced products

We are a delivery service bringing local produce and products straight to your doorstep! For hundreds of years ancient hawaiians used the land to sustain their communities and its ohanas. We want to continue this tradition by inviting the community to support local farmers and businesses.

What does Maile Mākeke mean? “Maile” is a vine that is endemic to the islands of hawaii. Maile is most commonly used in hawaiian lei to represent love, respect, endurance, and devotion. Mākeke is the hawaiian word for market, as we want to create a stress free and online produce market. Like a vine, our company is a connection between farmers and ohana. We grow and spread our vines throughout the community to deliver fresh and healthy produce. By partnering with these businesses we can create a more sustainable and community driven island.
